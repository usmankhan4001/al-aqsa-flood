## 🛠️ Quick Tips
- **Sticky Navigation**: Use the bottom bar to quickly switch between the Library, Highlights, Notes, and Studio.
- **RTL Support**: The app logic is fully optimized for Urdu, including page navigation and layout direction.
- **Attached Menus**: Drawers feel "attached" for a more professional and premium experience.
